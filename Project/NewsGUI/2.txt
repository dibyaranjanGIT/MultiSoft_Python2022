The NBA boycotts the call to the bar for Olanipekun’s refusal to resign as president of the Body of Benchers
December 6, 2022073
the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA), on Tuesday, stayed away from the induction ceremony for new lawyers in Abuja.

The absence of NBA representatives at the event, where the new members were admitted to their association, was a protest against Wole Olanipekun’s refusal to heed the request to resign as president of the Body of Benchers (BoB).
