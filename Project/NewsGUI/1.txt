Tech-skilled staff shortage a big threat to 58% of businesses in Nigeria: survey

A Nigerian business survey has shown that 58 per cent of IT decision makers in Nigeria view the shortage of IT skilled staff as one of the main threats to their business.

The survey also found that 34 per cent of IT decision makers in Nigeria plan to move to a new region and 33 per cent to a new country, while 58 per cent see the shortage of staff with IT skills as a of the main threats to your business
